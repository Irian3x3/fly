## Fun
- ### clyde
> Args: message (type: text)
>
> Usage: clyde &lt;message&gt;
>
> Description: Makes clyde say something
- ### youtube
> Args: search (type: text)
>
> Usage: youtube &lt;search&gt;
>
> Description: Searches YouTube for your query.
>
> Aliases: yt
